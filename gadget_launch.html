Features

  • Ultra-fast SSD for lightning-fast loading times
  • Ray tracing for realistic graphics
  • 4K gaming with HDR support
  • DualSense wireless controller with haptic feedback
  • 3D audio technology for immersive sound
  • Backward compatibility with PS4 games
  • Support for PlayStation VR

Specifications

  • CPU: 8-core AMD Zen 2 at 3.5GHz
  • GPU: AMD RDNA 2, 10.28 TFLOPs, Ray Tracing
  • RAM: 16GB GDDR6
  • Storage: 825GB NVMe SSD
  • Optical Drive: Ultra HD Blu-ray (66G/100G)
  • Video Output: 4K/8K, HDR, up to 120Hz
  • Audio: Tempest 3D AudioTech
  • Connectivity: USB Type-A, USB Type-C, HDMI 2.1, Ethernet, Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.1

Launch

The PlayStation 5 was officially launched on November 12, 2020 in select regions, including the US, Japan, Canada, and Australia. It became available worldwide on November 19, 2020. The launch marked a new era in gaming with advanced hardware, exclusive titles, and innovative features.

In India, the PS5 was launched on February 2, 2021, with limited stock available initially. The console received a strong response from Indian gamers and quickly sold out during its first pre-order phase.
